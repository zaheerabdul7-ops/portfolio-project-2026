## Post 1 — June 18, 2026

A'lotta sales leaders make deal reviews about what they need to GET.
But a deal review is actually about what we GIVE our reps.

Done right, the rep is our customer, and our "deliverable" is a
creative idea or insight they couldn't have gotten without us.

It only counts as a deliverable if you give them a headstart:
- Write the intro
- Send the text
- Edit the deck

**Re-Engagement Email Framework (works 80% of the time):**

Subject: Idea, [Job To Be Done]

[Prospect], last we spoke, [1. their challenge in their words] was
a focus.

I was just running [2. content / resource / tool] and thought of you.

Are you still working on [3. goal related to the challenge]?

If so, check it out [4. link]. From what I'm seeing, I have an idea
for how you could use it, based on [customer proof / peer example].

Want to run through it?

---

## Key Insight
Stop making outreach about what YOU need.
Make it about what VALUE you can give the prospect upfront.
Bring a new idea, a framework, and help them draft and ship it.
Buyer-centric selling = more replies + faster deals.
