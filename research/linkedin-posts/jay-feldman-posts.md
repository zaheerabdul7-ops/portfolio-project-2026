## Post 1 — June 24, 2026

I tried a new lead magnet strategy in my cold email campaigns and went 
from 2% to 8% reply rates. Here's exactly what changed.

**The Problem:** Traditional lead magnets like PDFs and checklists stopped 
working. Prospects see through them because everyone pumps them out with AI.

**The Solution — SaaS Magnet System:**

1. Scrapped generic "reply for free guide" CTA entirely
2. Used Consulty AI's free lead magnet builder to generate a PRD 
   (product requirements document)
3. Uploaded PRD into Bolt.new — converts to a working app with live link
4. Connected Apify ($10 per 1,000 profiles) to scrape LinkedIn data
5. Used OpenRouter.ai as LLM API for personalized analysis
6. CTA shifted to: "I looked at your LinkedIn and built custom angles 
   for you. Want me to send it over?"
7. n8n automatically sends personalized link when prospect replies yes
8. Added Facebook, Google, LinkedIn, TikTok pixels for retargeting

**Results:** Client "Gundi" hit 8% reply rate — had to PAUSE campaigns 
due to too many responses.

**Cost:** Less than $1 per lead. Built by a complete beginner using free tools.

Old way = hope a PDF is enough.
New way = show up with work already done before conversation starts.
