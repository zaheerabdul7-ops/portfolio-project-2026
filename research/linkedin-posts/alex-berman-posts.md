## Post 1 — June 2026

I gained 1,240 LinkedIn followers in 2 weeks (and woke up my dying account)

**Key Framework — How to Grow on LinkedIn in 2026:**

**1. Pretend You Have No Followers**
When you act like you're starting from scratch, you push yourself to
make every word and every idea count. Your followers mean nothing.

**2. Spend a Lot More Time Writing**
Average post now takes 3 HOURS from beginning to end.
Start with raw ideas in a voice note, then write, edit, rewrite line
by line. Write 3 hooks before picking one — never go with first instinct.

**3. Giveaways Are Key**
Pull best-performing content from X or YouTube and reframe as giveaway.
"GPT Cold Email Secrets" giveaway got 93 comments in 24 hours —
every commenter followed.

If a post gets fewer than 10 comments in first 30 minutes → delete
and pivot.

**The Funnel:**
Giveaway → Email list → "Buy from me" pitch

Results: 1,000 net new email subscribers + 120 new applications
in just 2 weeks.

## Key Insight
Cold outreach + LinkedIn content work together as a funnel.
Content builds audience → giveaways convert to email list →
email list converts to clients.
