## Post 1 — Via Gabriel Lopez (Practitioner applying Justin's method)
**Source:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/gabriel-metiche/
**Date:** June 19, 2026

Gabriel Lopez applied Justin Michael's outreach methodology with
impressive results.

**The Method — Multiple Touchpoints in Short Period:**
- A call
- A voicemail
- An email
- Another call
- Another voicemail

Goal is NOT to overwhelm the prospect.
Goal is FAMILIARITY.

Every touch increases chances prospect recognizes:
- Your name
- Your company
- Your phone number

**Results Gabriel Got:**
Average duration of conversations with ICPs increased by nearly 80%.
Prospects staying on phone longer AND sharing more useful information —
which helps create personalized follow-ups aligned with their
actual priorities.

---

## Key Insight
"The first objective isn't to get a meeting.
The first objective is to become recognizable.

Recognition → Familiarity → Conversations → Opportunities"

Multi-touch sequences work because familiarity builds trust
before the pitch ever happens.
