## The 3 Pillars of Cold Email

### Pillar 1 — Technical Infrastructure
- Buy 3-5 secondary domains (never use main domain)
- Set up SPF, DKIM, DMARC on every domain
- Warm up domains minimum 30 days before sending
- Use Google mailboxes (87.2% inbox placement)
- Keep bounce rate below 2%
- Tools: Instantly AI, SmartLead

### Pillar 2 — List Building
- Use Apollo.io for B2B data (best balance of cost + accuracy)
- Always filter by: Job Title, Industry, Location,
  Company Size, Email Status (Verified)
- Use company keywords — more specific than industry filters
- Target decision makers (C-suite), NOT mid-level employees
- Target younger companies (less vendor loyalty)
- Target newly hired executives (they want change)
- Verify emails with Million Verifier
- Verify catchall emails with Find Email

### Pillar 3 — Offer & Copy
- 6 sentences max. 6th grade reading level
- Triple Tap Framework:
  1. Preview text — get the OPEN
  2. Body — get them to READ
  3. CTA — get them to REPLY with one word
- Never signal the sale in subject line
- Use spin tax (mandatory for deliverability)
- Follow-up sequence: Day 0, Day 3, Day 7 (max 3 emails)

- Upload Apollo list to Clay table

### Signal Workflows
- Job postings signal: Company is willing to invest in solution

- Gets 3-4% reply rates vs 1-3% traditional
