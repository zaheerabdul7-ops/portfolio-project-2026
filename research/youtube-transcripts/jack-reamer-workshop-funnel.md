## Core Concept — The Workshop Funnel

### Why Old Cold Email Fails
Everyone does the same thing:
1. Scrape a list
2. Send generic message
3. Try to book a call immediately
4. Follow up aggressively

Problem: You're asking someone to marry you without dating first.
No relationship. No trust. No proof you can solve their problem.

### The Workshop Funnel — New Way

Instead of asking for a 15-minute call → Invite them to a FREE
live workshop/training on how to get a specific outcome they want.

**Step 1 — Build Highly Targeted List**
Not every agency owner with a pulse.
Target: Specific agency + specific pain point.
Quality beats quantity.

**Step 2 — Send Workshop Invitation Email**
Framework: Case Study + New Method + Invite

Example email:
"We just helped Michael add $10,000 in new revenue using a super
simple method that agency owners haven't implemented yet.
We're launching a workshop to go over this method so you can start
predictably growing your business.
Reply YES and I'll send you the link to register."

Just 4 lines. One sentence each. Gets 3-4% reply rates.

**Step 3 — Run the Workshop**
Title format: "How to go from X to Y using [Unique Mechanism]"

Workshop structure:
- Intro + credibility
- 3 pillars: Situation → Problem → Implication → Teach → Case Study
- Offer at the end: "Is it cool if I share how we help people like you?"

**Step 4 — The Math**
5,000 emails sent:
- 200 replies (4%)
- 100 positive replies
- 50 workshop attendees
- 10 booked calls (20% of attendees)
- 3 closed deals (30% close rate)
- $15,000 revenue (at $5K avg deal)

## Key Insight
Sell ONE to MANY instead of one to one.
Workshop pre-warms prospects + positions you as authority +
eliminates skepticism before the sales call.
Consumer trust is at all-time low — authority positioning is
more important than ever.
