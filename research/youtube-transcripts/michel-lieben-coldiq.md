## Key Insights from Video

### 1. Start With Your Best Skill
Use the skill you're selling to find your first clients.
Michel used cold email to find cold email clients — this is your own
proof of concept.

### 2. Agency Growth Timeline
- Jan 2023 — Started fulltime, 0 clients
- Feb 2023 — First client at $3K/month
- May 2023 — 6 clients, $15.7K/month
- Oct 2023 — $30K/month
- July 2024 — $150K/month
- Sept 2024 — $200K/month
- Jan 2025 — $300K/month

### 3. LinkedIn Content = Biggest Growth Driver
- Posted daily on LinkedIn for 3+ months with zero results
- One viral post (146K impressions) changed everything
- Content builds trust that makes cold outreach convert better
- Team posting generated $80K MRR in 90 days

### 4. Clay Changed Everything
- Was paying 5 people to personalize first lines manually
- Clay's AI agent did same work in 15 minutes
- Clay became central piece of their outbound stack

### 5. High Intent Audience Strategy
Combine content + outbound:
- Someone likes your post → they're warm
- Send DM: "Saw you engaged with my post, curious what caught your attention"
- Not pitching — just starting a conversation
- This generated 162 meetings in ONE month

### 6. Cold Outreach Tech Stack
- Clay — personalization + list building
- Secondary domains — email deliverability
- LinkedIn ads — amplify proven organic posts
- Attention — sales call recording + coaching
